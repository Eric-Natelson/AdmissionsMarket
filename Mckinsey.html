Welcome!

Every year McKinsey & Company sends a cohort of consultants to top MBA programs around the world and this year it’s your turn. You’re likely dreaming of gaining admittance to your dream school and working hard to make it a reality. For many of you a large part of the process will be selecting (and working with) the right admissions consultant.

AdmissionsMarket serves as a matchmaker to connect MBA applicants to the top admissions consultants in the world – no matter the admissions firm they work with. We’ve spent countless hours vetting over 500 of the world’s top consultants, and even developed preferential pricing for McKinsey consultants

Check out our site and reach out if you’d like to learn more.




Kirsten, Co-founder

YOUR FUTURE DESERVES THE BEST

AdmissionsMarket finds the best MBA admissions consultant for you

We stood where you are today and didn’t know who to trust…

AdmissionsMarket was created by a Wharton grad to offer unbiased and educated advice in an industry where applicants don’t know what firms to believe and where so many consultants seem the same.

…so we shined a light on the industry to design a better path for you...

We review profiles from hundreds of MBA admissions consultants, curate the best, vet their backgrounds, and study over a dozen data points to ensure you only get the best.

We’re committed to working side-by-side with you…

You’ll work hand-in-hand with one of our committed personal concierges who will listen to your goals, walk through what really matters in the consultant selection process, share common pitfalls, and ultimately use our database to match you to the best consultant for you.

...and when you succeed, so does our community

The truth is that the admissions consulting industry can have adverse impacts on our community – we’re making those that can afford it more competitive which risks further marginalizing the economically disadvantaged. That’s why we mentor a first-generation college applicant for every client we work with.
