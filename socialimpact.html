OUR SOCIAL IMPACT MODEL

At AdmissionsMarket we don’t just want to assist a few high schoolers to get into college– we want to disrupt the cycle of poverty by finding a sustainable solution to mentorship. This is why our social impact goals are fully interwoven into our business model. Here’s how it works:
We offer three hours of pro-bono coaching to a qualifying college applicant, for every client that signs with a consultant through our site
  • We make it free to work with us so as to encourage as many prospective applicants as possible to use our platform to sign with an admissions consulting firm. The more clients we get, the more we can give to others.
  • We recognize a lot of students need help so we prioritize our pro-bono cases based on the options we have on the table. Historically, we’ve focused on first-generation college applicants but are open to helping anyone in need.
    • If you’re interested – We invite you to be part of the solution by finding easy ways for you to help too
  • You’ll receive a short bio of the beneficiary that you sponsored by signing with a consultant through us. Then if you’re interested, we will offer to provide you updates during his/her key educational milestones (e.g. when your beneficiary applies to school, gets accepted, enrolls) and/or even put you in touch if you’d like to help mentor him/her yourself.
  • In order to maximize our social impact we need clients, which is why we tie additional pro-bono support to your referrals. For every client you refer to AdmissionsMarket, we’ll provide an additional two hours of support to your beneficiary.
    • Here’s the type of support you can expect your beneficiary to receive in thanks for signing with a consultant through AdmissionsMarket:
  • The “standard” three hours of coaching: An assessment of his/her profile & school selection; high level essay brainstorming and time for a Q&A session Additional coaching based on client referrals:
  • Additional coaching based on client referrals:
  • 1 Referral: In-depth essay brainstorming, One-time review of one essay for his/her dream school, General Q&A
  • 2 Referrals: One additional review of up to three essays for his/her dream school
  • 3 Referrals: Two rounds of reviews of up to three essays for a second school
  • 4 Referrals: We’ll roll-over additional support to a new beneficiary!
